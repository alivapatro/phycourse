day-wise plan

Detailed 50-days JEE physics day-wise study plan which ensures comprehensive coverage of the syllabus,balancing theory and problem-solving practice.adjustment can be made based on individual progress and specific needs

Introduction to Physics and Basic Mathematics

  • Units and Dimensions: Understanding fundamental units and derived units, dimensional analysis.
  • Vectors: Scalars, vectors, vector addition, subtraction, and multiplication

Kinematics

  • Motion in one dimension, velocity, acceleration, graphical analysis of motion, equations of motion.
  • Projectile motion, relative velocity, uniform circular motion.

Laws of motion

  • newton's laws:Understanding the fundamental laws governing motion
  • friction:types of friction and laws governing friction.
  • dynamics of circular motion

Work,energy and power

  • Work done by a force,kinetic and potential energy,work-energy theorem
  • conservation of energy,power

system of particles

  • The concept of center of mass for a system of particles
  • Collision types and their analysis.

rotational motion

  • Basic concepts of rotational motion, moment of inertia, and rotational kinematics.
  • Dynamics of rotational motion, torque, angular momentum, and conservation laws.

gravitation

  • Understanding the gravitational force and its effects.
  • Newton’s law of universal gravitation, orbital velocity, gravitational potential energy, escape velocity.

mechanics revision

  • Revision and Practice Problems

Fluids and properties of matter

  • fluid mechanics:buoyancy, Bernoulli’s principle,surface tension, viscosity.
  • Elasticity:stress-strain, Young’s modulus

KTG and Heat transfer

  • kinetic thoery of gases:Ideal gas laws, pressure, temperature, volume relationships, Maxwell-Boltzmann distribution.
  • heat transfer:Temperature, heat, specific heat, calorimetry, thermal expansion.

Laws of thermodynamics

  • Zeroth law, first law, specific heats, second law, Carnot engine, entropy.

oscilations and waves

  • Simple harmonic motion (SHM), energy in SHM, damped oscillations.
  • Types of waves, wave properties (speed, frequency, wavelength), superposition, interference.
  • Sound waves

electrostatics

  • Charge, Coulomb’s law, electric field and electric potential
  • capacitance

current electricity

  • Electric current, resistance, Ohm’s law, series and parallel circuits, Kirchhoff’s laws.
  • revise previous topics

magnetism

  • Magnetic fields, forces on charged particles, Biot-Savart law, Ampere’s law.

Electromagnetic induction

  • How changing magnetic fields induce electric currents.
  • Faraday's law, Lenz's law, inductance, AC and DC generators.

alternatig current and EM waves

  • AC circuits and their properties.AC vs. DC, impedance, reactance, power in AC circuits
  • Study of waves formed by oscillating electric and magnetic fields.Electromagnetic spectrum, propagation, applications.

  • Mirrors, lenses, optical instruments.

wave optics

Atoms and nuclei
